## Tourism Overview

Following the lifting of travel restrictions and revived traveler confidence, Finland has seen a steady recovery in domestic tourism.  

While the number of visitors may not have drastically surged, the **total revenue rose to ‚Ç¨2.21 billion in 2024**‚Äîa clear sign that travelers are spending more per visit.
